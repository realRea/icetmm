About The Conference

Magnetism has been a fascinating field of research from a long time. Materials exhibiting this phenomenon not only have applications important for mankind but also needs detailed investigation for understanding of underlying mechanism. Complication in this arises when the size of these materials reduces to nanoregime. However magnetic materials with nanodimension are the base for futuristcs applications that may be boon to mankind. For example, super paramagnetic magnetic nanoparticles have been utilized for magnetic drug delivery; devices based on these materials are utilized in developing magnetic random access memories. Scaling down upto nanoscale gives rise to further complicated phenomen like spin flittering, spin tunneling, do ferromagnetism which can never be though in classical magnetism. At international level recent development some remarkable progress has been made in this field, however at national level not much progress has been made. Hence, there is need to provide a platform for scientific community and expert in this fascinating field of research so that a discussion on the recent development, new magnetic phenomena occurring at nanoscale, their utilization in applications and issues related with new involved technology may be discussed.
